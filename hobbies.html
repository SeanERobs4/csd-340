Hobbies About Home

Artwork

Jon has always been inspired by abstract artists like Andy Warhol, and M. C. Escher. He has showcased his work in various art shows throughout the years. Besides his main illustrated work, he's developed his own little collectable figure line he calls "ZR8" (Pictured in Figure 4).

He even has his own website called JPC Gallery, where he can display and offer these original art, illustrations, and one of a kind product designs. Available for sale on the site right now, he has custom skins for the SP-404 MKII, a beat-making, sample-slicing instrument, as well as custom PO boxes each with their own unique designs.

Other Hobbies

Jon has many other hobbies outside of his art. In fact, he may even find as much joy in music as he does with visual art. He's performed in public before, but also enjoys recording music of his own using his special keyboard and other equipment.

Other interests of his include nature photography, field-recording, kayaking, and cycling. He also loves spending time with his brothers by going on adventures, recording music, and playing video games.
